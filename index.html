About Me

Hello! I'm Lindsey. I'm a digital marketer at a small HubSpot Partner agency in Central Florida, and I'm slowly but surely making my way though UCF's Coding Boot Camp. I'm jazzed to be learning more about web development!

I started my career as a community college public speaking instructor, worked for some time in corporate communciations and sales training at a large insurance agency, and then landed at my current agency as agency director.
